useful keyboard shortcuts for win 11:
```text
switch subwindows and choose app:                           windows + tab
change current app layout:                                  windows + arrow keys
screen snapshot:                                            windows + shift + s
reopen closed windows (edge):                               ctrl + shift + t
show clear desktop during press the key composition:        alt + ,
show win emoji during press the key compositioin:           alt + .
when IT forcibly restart your pc, try typing in cmdline:    shutdown -a to postpone it
```
